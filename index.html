Discover adorable animals we sell for domestication purposes to our clients.

Cats

Cats are gentle and independent pets that make wonderful companions. They are playful, curious, and known for their cleanliness. With proper care and love, cats can live long and healthy lives.

Ducks

Ducks are friendly domesticated birds often raised for eggs and meat. They are social animals that enjoy living in groups and need access to clean water. With proper care, ducks can be healthy, active, and productive on farms or in backyards.

Dogs

Dogs are loyal and friendly companions known for their intelligence and protective nature. They thrive on love, training, and regular exercise. With proper care, dogs become devoted members of the family.

Hens

Hens are domesticated birds commonly raised for their nutritious eggs. They are social animals that do best in clean, safe environments with proper feeding. With good care, hens remain healthy and productive.
